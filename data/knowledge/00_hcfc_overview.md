# Huntsville City FC — Overview (Last updated: 2025-11-01)

**Huntsville City Football Club (HCFC)** is a professional soccer team in **MLS NEXT Pro** (the MLS developmental league).  
- **City:** Huntsville, Alabama  
- **League:** MLS NEXT Pro  
- **Founded:** 2022 (announced July 12, 2022)  
- **First season:** 2023  
- **Parent club / Owner:** **Nashville SC** (HCFC is Nashville’s MLS NEXT Pro affiliate)  
- **Home venue:** **Wicks Family Field at Joe Davis Stadium** (Huntsville), capacity ~6,000  
- **Nickname:** “Boys in Blue”  
- **Current head coach (2025):** **Chris O’Neal**

**What HCFC is for:** develop players, coaches, and staff in a competitive pro environment; provide a pathway from academy/Next Pro to MLS first teams (here, Nashville SC).

## Why this matters to fans and players
- Local access to pro soccer in North Alabama  
- Player pathway: standout HCFC players can earn opportunities/trials with Nashville SC  
- Community identity: events and themes (e.g., “Space Night”) tie the club to Huntsville culture
