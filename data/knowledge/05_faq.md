# HCFC — Quick FAQ

**What league is this?**  
**MLS NEXT Pro**, a professional league operated by MLS that bridges academies and first teams.

**Who owns/operates HCFC?**  
Affiliated with and owned by **Nashville SC** (MLS). HCFC is Nashville’s pro reserve/development side.

**Where do they play?**  
**Wicks Family Field at Joe Davis Stadium**, Huntsville (capacity ~6,000).

**When did HCFC start?**  
Announced in 2022; first season 2023.

**What are big match-day themes?**  
“**Space Night**” is the most iconic, reflecting Rocket City culture.

**Can players move to Nashville SC?**  
Yes. Standouts can sign with or be called to Nashville SC as roster rules allow.

**How do I get tickets?**  
The club’s site lists season packages and single-game tickets (including special theme nights).
