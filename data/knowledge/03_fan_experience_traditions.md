# Fan Experience, Traditions & Themes

## “Space Night”
A signature HCFC theme night celebrating Huntsville’s aerospace heritage (Marshall Space Flight Center, Artemis, etc.).  
- Activities have included NASA/space exhibits, themed inflatables, and special giveaways.  
- In 2025, HCFC unveiled an **all-orange third kit** inspired by spaceflight **ACES** suits during Space Night.

## Supporter culture (general)
- Supporters’ sections bring drums, chants, and tifos.  
- Family-friendly match-day with on-field and concourse activations.

## Rivalries / regional intensity
- Regional match-ups in the Eastern Conference fuel atmosphere (e.g., vs **Chattanooga FC**, **Orlando City B**, etc.).  
- Proximity matches tend to draw larger crowds and louder sections.
