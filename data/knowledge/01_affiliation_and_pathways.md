# Affiliation & Player Pathways

**Affiliation:** HCFC is the **MLS NEXT Pro** affiliate of **Nashville SC (MLS)**.  
- Contractually and operationally linked: Nashville SC holds roster/build decisions and can move players between the MLS and NEXT Pro rosters subject to league rules.

## Typical player movement patterns
- **Upward:** Strong HCFC performers can be signed or short-term called into Nashville SC (for first-team training, Leagues Cup, U.S. Open Cup, or MLS matches as rules permit).  
- **Rehab / Minutes:** Nashville SC may send first-team players to HCFC for match fitness.  
- **Academy to Pro:** Prospects may progress Academy → Next Pro → MLS.

## Examples (illustrative)
- Nashville SC has signed players from HCFC when they’ve impressed in MLS NEXT Pro competition or training environments (e.g., 2025: defender **Jordan Knight** earned an MLS deal after time with HCFC).
