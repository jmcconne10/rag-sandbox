# HCFC Seasons Snapshot (2023–2025)

> Notes: MLS NEXT Pro uses evolving competition formats (including shootouts for draws in some seasons). Always check the current year’s rules.

## 2023 (Inaugural Season)
- First home match at Joe Davis Stadium: **May 19, 2023** (sold out; win vs Crown Legacy FC).  
- Established fan base and opened renovated stadium to soccer.

## 2024
- Eastern Conference finish outside playoff places; continued roster development and player movement with Nashville SC.

## 2025
- Head Coach: **Chris O’Neal**.  
- **Milestone:** **First-ever MLS NEXT Pro playoff berth** (since club debut in 2023), clinched late in the season with a decisive result vs **Orlando City B**.  
- Notables (regular season highlights): strong home performances, themed nights (e.g., Space Night), growing average attendance.
