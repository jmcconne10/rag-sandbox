# Wicks Family Field at Joe Davis Stadium (Huntsville)

- **Official name:** Wicks Family Field at **Joe Davis Stadium**  
- **Location:** 3125 Don Mincher Dr SW (also listed as Leeman Ferry Rd), Huntsville, AL 35801  
- **Owner:** City of Huntsville  
- **Capacity:** ~6,000 for soccer  
- **Opened for soccer:** May 19, 2023 (after renovation; originally a 1985 baseball park)  
- **Primary tenant:** Huntsville City FC (MLS NEXT Pro), since 2023  
- **Surface:** Bermudagrass (Tifway 419)  
- **Notable feature:** ~67' jumbotron; venue used for soccer and multi-use events (football, rugby, lacrosse, etc.).

## Fan logistics (quick)
- Expect paid parking and typical stadium policies (clear bags, security, etc.).  
- Concessions and family sections vary by match; check HCFC match-day emails or the club site for specifics.  
- Private/group events are supported via stadium rentals.
